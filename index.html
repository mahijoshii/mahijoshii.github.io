Long-term changes in temperature and weather patterns are referred to as climate change. Human activities have been the primary cause of climate change since the 1800s, mostly as a result of burning fossil fuels like coal, oil, and gas. People are at risk from climate change because it will lead to a lack of food and water, increased flooding, severe storms, loss of species, high heat, an increase in disease, poverty, and economic loss.

Our mission

Only One Earth is working to bring awareness to the climate issue and educate individuals on the topic. We hope to see changes in out society which will benefit the planet and reduce the effects of global warming. On this website, you can learn about how global warming is affecting the planet, contributors to climate change, along with what you can do to help!
